Our Story

Welcome to Chrome & Burger, where passion for food meets the open road. We started as a small food truck with a big dream: to serve the most delicious, handcrafted burgers you've ever tasted. Our secret? We use only the freshest, locally-sourced ingredients and a whole lot of love. Our truck is our kitchen, and the city is our dining room. Come find us and taste the difference!
